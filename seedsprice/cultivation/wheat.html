Wheat cultivation method

Cultivation method:

  • High / medium high land required.
  • 4-5 plowing should be done to loosen the soil and free the weeds
  • Sow the seeds after 1 ft.
  • Sow seeds in rows 2 feet apart.
  • Application of fertilizers (DAP, MOP, Gypsum) in final tillage.
  • 1st installment application of Sahar (Urea) after 20 days.
  • 2nd installment of fertilizer application (DAP, Urea, Zinc, MOP) 1 month after 1st installment.
  • At the end of the installment, the soil should be lined at the base of the tree.
  • Irrigation should be given as required.

    • Mustard cultivation method

    Cultivation method:

  • The land should be prepared with 4-6 plowing/ladders.
  • Vitavex-200/Canton Sahar should be applied before sowing the seeds.
  • Once 15-20 days after sowing seeds and once at the time of flowering.
  • (Fertilizers should be applied at planting stage (Urea, TSP, Gypsum, Zinc Sulphate, Dolochun, Balanced Compost). Urea should be applied 1-2 times within 30 to 45 days of sowing, Urea and other fertilizers should be applied during land preparation, Urea The field should be weeded and irrigated before application.
  • First irrigation should be given within 25-30 days of sowing (before flowering) and second irrigation within 50-55 days (at fruiting time). If there is less moisture in the soil at the time of sowing, a light irrigation should be given within 10-15 days of seedling emergence Fertilizer should be given more if irrigation is assured.
